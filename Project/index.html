Impact of COVID-19

In 2019, the world was confronted with a global crisis that would reshape the way we live, work, and learn.

COVID-19 pandemic swept across the globe, marking the start of a new era of uncertainty and challenges. Overnight, people had to adapt to a lifestyle in which staying safe from the virus required significant changes to our daily routines.

Adults found themselves working from home, students transitioned to remote learning, and the fundamental nature of collaboration underwent a transformation.

Gabriel Arias
